Frequently Asked Questions

Explore answers to common queries about our professional electrical services, pricing, safety standards, and more.

What types of electrical services do you offer?

+

We provide comprehensive electrical services, including installations, repairs, full rewiring, energy-efficient lighting solutions, safety inspections, and commercial electrical projects. We also specialize in smart home integrations and EV charging station installations.

Are your electricians certified and insured?

+

Absolutely. Our electricians are NICEIC-certified, TrustMark-accredited, and fully insured. We adhere to BS 7671 wiring regulations and ensure all work meets UK safety standards.

Do you offer 24/7 emergency services?

+

Yes, we provide round-the-clock emergency electrical services. Call 0800 123 4567 for immediate assistance with power outages, exposed wiring, or other urgent issues.

How do I book a service appointment?

+

You can book online via our website’s booking portal or call us at 0800 123 4567. We offer flexible scheduling, including same-day appointments when available, and provide transparent quotes upfront.

What areas do you cover?

+

We serve clients across the UK, with our headquarters in Manchester. Our team operates in major cities and surrounding areas. Contact us to confirm service availability in your location.

How long does an electrical project typically take?

+

Timelines depend on project complexity. Minor repairs take a few hours, while rewiring a home may take 3–7 days. Commercial projects vary widely. We provide detailed timelines during our initial assessment.

How is pricing determined?

+

Pricing is based on project scope, materials, labor, and complexity. We offer free, no-obligation quotes after an on-site or virtual consultation. Transparent pricing with no hidden fees is our guarantee.

Do you provide warranties for your work?

+

Yes, we offer a 12-month warranty on labor and up to 5 years on select installations, depending on the project. Manufacturer warranties apply to materials, and we provide full documentation.

Can you install energy-efficient solutions?

+

We specialize in energy-efficient solutions, including LED lighting, smart thermostats, and solar panel integrations. These options reduce energy costs and environmental impact. Ask about our eco-friendly packages.

What should I do to prepare for your visit?

+

Clear the work area of furniture or obstacles, ensure access to electrical panels, and inform us of any specific issues. For safety, keep pets and children away from the work zone during our visit.
